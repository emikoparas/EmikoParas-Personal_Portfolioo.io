About Me

Hello there, My name is Emiko. I study creative computing at Bathspa University as a 19-year-old student.

I was born in Makati, Manila, Philippines, on October 31, 2004. The ability of computers to foster creativity and innovation was the source of my early fascination with them. My preferred digital expression techniques have evolved into video editing, game coding, and website design. As I've gotten older, my love for computers has only gotten stronger because of the ever-evolving tools and technologies that let us express ourselves and communicate. I believe that computers are more than just tools; they are platforms for creativity, inventors, and boundless possibilities.

Throughout my academic career, I have experimented with a wide range of subjects, including computer science, mathematics, and art. The way that creativity and technology can work together to solve difficult problems and spark original thought is something I find particularly fascinating. Through my studies, I've learned the importance of multidisciplinary perspectives and the necessity of maintaining an open mind and a curious mind.

In addition to my academic work, I enjoy engaging in a wide range of extracurricular activities and hobbies. These include going on walks in the outdoors, playing basketball, being by yourself, taking pictures and videos, and maintaining a regular exercise routine. These activities improve my overall health because they give me a break from my academic responsibilities and rekindle my passion for learning. As I strive to get better every day, they give me chances for both personal development and relaxation.

As I advance in my academic endeavors, I'm excited to take advantage of new chances for growth and learning. With the help of this website, I can connect with people who share my interests and goals, share my ideas, and keep track of my educational journey.
