My Hobbies

I engage in a range of physically and mentally taxing activities when I have free time. My competitive spirit is fueled by my love of basketball, and regular exercise keeps me energized. Making videos fulfills my creative needs, whether I'm just recording impromptu moments or editing footage. I love to keep memories alive, so I constantly take pictures of my trips with my phone. I discover that keeping a journal enables me to reflect on my everyday experiences, objectives, and ideas as well as express myself therapeutically. I value the variety of chances these activities provide for personal development and exploration in general.
