The Story

When I was little, my family left the Philippines on what was initially intended to be a vacation. However, our father, who had been working in the UAE for more than 20 years, decided to move us there in search of better educational opportunities and a safer environment because of my mother's difficulties juggling her career and raising three children my sister, brother, and me. I've been studying in the UAE since elementary school and am currently in my first year of college. At first, being so young, I felt anxious and culture shocked, not knowing what was in store for me. Nevertheless, I eventually adjusted, picked up the kinks, and became comfortable in the new setting. I'm grateful for my father's efforts to provide for our family and to pave the way for my future in the United Arab Emirates, a country that, in contrast to the Philippines, offers a wealth of opportunities as well as security.
